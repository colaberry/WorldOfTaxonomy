[IB] IAB Content Categories -- The IB entry serves as the top-level grouping for the IAB Tech Lab Content Taxonomy, encompassing all defined IAB content categories used for ad targeting, measurement, and analytics across digital media. It organizes the detailed sub-categories under a single umbrella, enabling consistent classification of website and app content for industry-wide reporting.
[IB.01] Arts and Entertainment -- IB.01 Arts and Entertainment groups content that focuses on creative and leisure activities, including music, movies, television, theater, visual arts, literature, gaming, and related news or reviews. It covers both consumption and production of artistic works and entertainment media.
[IB.02] Automotive -- The IB.02 Automotive category includes content focused on motor vehicles and the broader transportation sector, covering topics such as vehicle reviews, news, maintenance advice, industry trends, and related technology and engineering developments.
[IB.03] Business and Finance -- IB.03 Business and Finance includes content about corporate news, banking, investing, personal finance, accounting, and economic analysis. It covers articles, videos, and podcasts that discuss market trends, financial planning, business strategies, and related financial services.
[IB.04] Careers -- The IB.04 "Careers" category groups content focused on employment and professional development. It includes articles, videos, and guides about job searching, resume writing, interview preparation, workplace advice, industry trends, and career advancement strategies.
[IB.05] Education -- IB.05 Education covers content focused on learning and instruction, including school curricula, tutoring, higher-education resources, e-learning platforms, educational research, and related advice or news. It pertains to any material that helps users acquire knowledge or develop skills.
[IB.06] Family and Parenting -- IB.06 Family and Parenting covers content focused on family dynamics and child-rearing, offering advice and information for parents on topics such as pregnancy, newborn care, education, discipline, and overall family relationships.
[IB.07] Food and Drink -- The IB.07 category groups content focused on food and drink, including recipes, cooking tips, restaurant reviews, beverage guides, and broader food-culture topics. It helps advertisers and publishers target audiences interested in culinary experiences and dining.
[IB.08] Health and Fitness -- IB.08 covers content focused on personal health, exercise, nutrition, wellness and medical advice. It includes articles, videos and other media that provide fitness tips, workout routines, diet guidance, disease prevention and health news.
[IB.09] Home and Garden -- IB.09 Home and Garden groups content focused on residential living spaces, including interior design, home improvement, décor, maintenance, and gardening or landscaping advice. It covers DIY projects, outdoor living, and tips for creating functional or aesthetic environments in and around the home.
[IB.10] News and Politics -- IB.10 includes content that reports on current events, government actions, elections, policy debates and political analysis. It covers news articles, opinion pieces, editorial commentary and any material that informs or discusses political institutions, public affairs, and the broader sociopolitical environment.
[IB.11] Personal Finance -- Personal Finance covers content offering advice, tips, and information for managing individual money matters such as budgeting, saving, investing, debt and credit management, tax preparation, and retirement planning.
[IB.12] Pets -- IB.12 Pets covers content focused on companion animals such as dogs, cats, birds, fish and small mammals, including topics like pet care, training, health, nutrition, grooming, adoption and lifestyle advice for owners. It also includes product reviews and pet-related entertainment.
[IB.13] Science -- IB.13 Science covers content that focuses on scientific disciplines, research findings, technical breakthroughs and discussions of natural phenomena. It includes articles, videos and podcasts about biology, physics, chemistry, environmental studies and related fields, catering to audiences seeking evidence-based information.
[IB.14] Shopping -- IB.14 Shopping covers content related to consumer goods purchasing, product reviews, price comparisons, and e-commerce platforms. It includes articles, videos, and guides that help users find, evaluate, and buy items online or in stores.
[IB.15] Sports -- IB.15 - Sports covers content focused on athletic activities, including news, scores, analysis, commentary, and feature stories about professional, collegiate, and recreational sports. The category also includes coverage of events, teams, athletes, and related equipment or training information.
[IB.16] Style and Fashion -- A collection of content focused on clothing, accessories, beauty, grooming and personal style, covering fashion trends, runway shows, street-wear, styling tips, fashion news, designer profiles and related consumer advice.
[IB.17] Technology and Computing -- IB.17 Technology and Computing covers content focused on the development, use, and impact of computers, software, hardware, networking, internet services, and emerging tech such as AI, cloud, and cybersecurity, as well as news, reviews, tutorials, and industry analysis related to these topics. It includes discussions of programming languages, device specifications, tech business trends, and consumer electronics.
[IB.18] Travel -- Travel includes content about trips, destinations, tourism, transportation and related services such as airline, hotel, cruise or rental car reviews, travel planning guides, itineraries, travel tips, cultural experiences, sightseeing attractions and travel-related news or commentary. It is classified under IAB Tech Lab's IB (Lifestyle) segment as entry IB.18.
[IB.19] Real Estate -- The IB.19 Real Estate category includes content focused on residential, commercial and industrial property topics such as buying, selling, leasing, financing, market analysis, development, and property management, as well as related legal and investment information.
[IB.20] Gaming -- IB.20 Gaming covers any material focused on video games and interactive entertainment, including game reviews, news, walkthroughs, e-sports coverage, streaming, hardware, and community discussions.
