[AT] AHTN Sections -- The AT code in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature designates the high-level sections that group goods by the first two digits of the HS code. These sections give a broad overview of product categories for customs, trade statistics and tariff policy across ASEAN member states.
[AT.I] Live Animals and Animal Products -- The AT.I section of the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups all live animals, from livestock, poultry and fish to other wildlife, together with animal products such as meat, dairy, eggs, honey, skins, wool and related items, for customs classification and trade statistics.
[AT.II] Vegetable Products -- AT.II in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups together all vegetable products, including fresh, chilled, frozen, dried, and processed forms such as pickles, sauces and purees. The entry covers a wide range of edible vegetables and their derivatives, regardless of the specific type of vegetable, but excludes items classified under more specific subheadings for roots, tubers or oil-seed products. It is used for customs duty assessments and trade statistics across ASEAN member states.
[AT.III] Fats and Oils -- AT.III includes all animal and vegetable fats and oils, whether crude, refined, partially hydrogenated or solidified, along with their primary derivatives such as margarine, shortenings, clarified butter and similar products used for food, industrial or cosmetic purposes.
[AT.IV] Prepared Foodstuffs -- AT.IV includes foods that have been processed, cooked, baked, fried, dried, fermented, canned or otherwise prepared for immediate or later consumption, such as ready-to-eat meals, snacks and ready-to-cook kits. Raw agricultural commodities and beverages are excluded.
[AT.IX] Wood and Cork -- AT.IX covers all natural wood and cork products, including timber, logs, sawn wood, veneers, wood panels, chips and sawdust, as well as unprocessed cork and bark used for manufacturing. The category excludes finished furniture, wooden packaging and wood-based composites, which are classified under separate headings.
[AT.V] Mineral Products -- AT.V refers to the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature category for mineral products, encompassing natural solid inorganic substances such as metal ores, industrial minerals, stone, sand, gravel, and related refined materials used in construction, manufacturing, and chemical processes. This grouping streamlines customs duties and trade statistics for raw and minimally processed mineral commodities across ASEAN member states.
[AT.VI] Chemical Products -- AT.VI in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups all chemical products, encompassing inorganic and organic chemicals, basic industrial chemicals, and compounds used in manufacturing, agriculture, and pharmaceuticals. The chapter covers raw chemicals, intermediates, and finished chemical goods, but excludes pharmaceuticals, cosmetics and food additives which are classified elsewhere.
[AT.VII] Plastics and Rubber -- Section AT.VII of the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups together all articles made from plastics and rubber, covering raw polymer resins, synthetic and natural rubber, and finished goods such as sheets, rods, tubes and molded products.
[AT.VIII] Hides and Leather -- AT.VIII covers raw, unprocessed hides and skins of animals, as well as finished leather and leather products that have been tanned, dressed or otherwise treated, including split, whole and reconstituted leather, but excludes footwear, garments and other articles made from leather.
[AT.X] Paper Products -- AT.X groups all paper products for ASEAN trade statistics, covering manufactured paper and paperboard items such as printing, writing, newsprint, tissue, and packaging paper that are not assigned to more specific headings. The code is used to apply uniform customs duties and monitor regional trade flows.
[AT.XI] Textiles -- AT.XI in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature designates the broad category of textiles, covering woven and knitted fabrics made from natural, synthetic or blended fibers but excluding finished garments, household textile articles and technical textile products. It is used for customs valuation, duty assessment and trade statistics across ASEAN member states.
[AT.XII] Footwear -- AT.XII in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature covers all types of footwear, including shoes, boots, sandals, and sports shoes, whether made of leather, textile, rubber, plastic or other materials, and specifies any associated parts such as uppers, soles and linings.
[AT.XIII] Stone, Ceramics, Glass -- AT.XIII in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups commodities made from natural stone, manufactured ceramic wares and articles, as well as glass and glassware, covering raw material forms, finished products and related items used in construction, household or industrial applications.
[AT.XIV] Precious Metals -- AT.XIV comprises gold, silver, platinum, palladium and other precious metals and their alloys, whether in bulk, ingots, granules or finished articles, intended for investment, jewelry or industrial use. The heading is applied in ASEAN customs to assess duties and compile trade
[AT.XV] Base Metals -- AT.XV in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups together primary base metals such as iron, steel, copper, aluminum and their alloys in raw, semi-processed or finished forms, covering commodities and products used in construction, manufacturing and other industrial sectors.
[AT.XVI] Machinery -- AT.XVI in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature covers all machinery, ranging from industrial and agricultural equipment to specialized machines, along with their parts and accessories. The chapter is used by ASEAN customs authorities for tariff assessment, statistical reporting and trade policy analysis.
[AT.XVII] Vehicles -- AT.XVII in the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature groups all motor vehicles designed for the transport of passengers or goods, whether powered by an engine, a motor or other means, and includes complete vehicles, chassis and related components not classified elsewhere, such as tractors, trucks, buses and special-purpose vehicles.
[AT.XVIII] Instruments -- AT.XVIII covers musical instruments, including string, wind, percussion and electronic devices used to produce sound, as well as their parts, accessories and repair kits. The heading applies to complete instruments and component items that are not classified elsewhere in the tariff schedule. It forms part of the ASEAN Harmonised Tariff Nomenclature for customs and trade reporting.
