[PA] Pantone Families -- PA designates the Pantone Families within the Pantone Color System, grouping the extensive palette into thematic categories such as solid coated, solid uncoated, pastel, metallic and specialty colors. These families help designers, printers and manufacturers organize, select and communicate precise colors across various applications.

[PA.02] Pantone Fashion, Home + Interiors -- PA.02 identifies the Pantone Fashion, Home + Interiors family, which supplies standardized color references for textiles, apparel, décor, paint and furnishings. The collection offers swatch guides and communication tools that enable designers, manufacturers and retailers to achieve consistent, repeatable colors across fashion, interior design and home-goods industries.
[PA.03] Pantone SkinTone Guide -- The Pantone SkinTone Guide is a curated set of standardized color swatches that represent a wide range of human skin tones, enabling designers, manufacturers, and artists to accurately match colors for cosmetics, apparel, medical and digital applications. It is listed as entry PA.03 within the Pantone Color System Families.
[PA.04] Pantone Pastels and Neons -- PA.04 identifies the Pantone Pastels and Neons family, a collection of low-saturation soft hues and high-intensity fluorescent shades defined in the Pantone Matching System. These colors are intended for applications requiring subtle or eye-catching visual impact, such as fashion, graphics, and product packaging.
[PA.05] Pantone Metallics -- PA.05 refers to the Pantone Metallics family within the Pantone Color System. This group comprises metallic shades that simulate the appearance of metal surfaces, used in graphic design, printing, and product packaging. The colors are formulated to provide a reflective, shimmering effect when applied with appropriate inks or coatings.
[PA.06] Pantone Extended Gamut -- Pantone Extended Gamut (PA.06) is a set of supplemental colors that expands the standard Pantone palette to include more saturated and vivid hues, especially useful for high-impact printing and digital work. It provides designers and manufacturers with a broader range of matching options while maintaining Pantone's precision and consistency across media.
[PA.07] Pantone Color Bridge -- PA.07 in the Pantone Color System Families refers to the Pantone Color Bridge, a reference guide that translates Pantone spot colors into their closest process equivalents for CMYK printing while also providing RGB, Hex and Lab values for digital use, allowing designers to match spot colors across media.
[PA.08] Pantone CMYK Guide -- PA.08, the Pantone CMYK Guide, is a printed reference that converts Pantone spot colors to their closest equivalents in the CMYK process palette. It provides swatches with CMYK values, allowing designers and printers to reproduce Pantone hues accurately on standard four-color presses without using specialty inks.
[PA.09] Color of the Year -- PA.09 in the Pantone Color System Families identifies the annual "Color of the Year" chosen by Pantone. Each year Pantone selects a hue that reflects cultural trends and guides designers, fashion houses, and product developers worldwide.
[PA.10] Pantone Connect (Digital) -- Pantone Connect (Digital) is an online subscription service that provides access to the Pantone Matching System in a digital format, allowing designers to view, organize, and apply Pantone colors across software and devices, and includes library syncing, spot color identification, and collaboration tools.
[PA.11] Pantone Plastics Standard -- PA.11 designates the Pantone Plastics Standard within the Pantone Color System Families, a reference guide that defines reproducible colors for plastic materials. It provides manufacturers with precise formulation data to achieve consistent hues across polymer products, ensuring reliable color matching in design and production.
