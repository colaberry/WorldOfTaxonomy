[JF] Job Families -- In the Generic Job Family Classification, the code JF designates "Job Families," a grouping of occupations that share similar tasks, skills, and knowledge requirements. This structure supports workforce analysis, talent management and career development by aligning related roles under common functional categories.
[JF.01] Executive/C-Suite -- JF.01 Executive/C-Suite denotes the top tier of organizational leadership, covering roles such as chief executive officer, chief financial officer, chief operating officer and other C-level officers. Holders of these positions set overall strategy, make major corporate decisions and bear ultimate accountability for performance and compliance.
[JF.02] Finance and Accounting -- JF.02 designates the Finance and Accounting job family, covering roles that handle financial reporting, budgeting, analysis, tax compliance, and treasury functions. It includes positions such as accountants, auditors, financial analysts, and controllers that support an organization's fiscal health.
[JF.03] Human Resources -- JF.03 in the Generic Job Family Classification designates the Human Resources job family, covering roles that manage workforce planning, recruiting, onboarding, employee relations, compensation and benefits, training, and compliance with labor regulations. Professionals in this family support organizational performance by aligning talent strategies with business goals.
[JF.04] Information Technology -- The JF.04 Information Technology job family groups occupations that design, develop, implement, maintain and support computer systems, software applications, networks and related infrastructure. It covers roles such as programmers, system administrators, IT analysts, security specialists and technology managers who ensure digital resources operate efficiently and securely.
[JF.05] Marketing and Communications -- JF.05 groups occupations that plan, create, and deliver messages to promote products, services, or an organization's image. It includes market research, advertising, branding, public relations, digital media, and internal communications roles. Professionals in this family develop strategies, produce content, and manage channels to reach target audiences.
[JF.06] Sales and Business Development -- The JF.06 Sales and Business Development job family includes roles that identify and acquire new customers, negotiate contracts, and drive revenue growth. Professionals develop market strategies, manage client relationships, and coordinate cross-functional initiatives to expand market share.
[JF.07] Operations and Supply Chain -- JF.07 Operations and Supply Chain groups occupations that plan, coordinate, and control the flow of materials, information, and services from suppliers to customers, including logistics, inventory management, procurement, production scheduling, and distribution activities. It covers roles that optimize efficiency, reduce costs, and ensure timely delivery across manufacturing and service environments.
[JF.08] Legal and Compliance -- JF.08, Legal and Compliance, groups occupations that provide advice on laws, regulations, and internal policies, draft and review contracts, manage litigation, and ensure organizational adherence to statutory and ethical standards. This job family includes lawyers, compliance officers, paralegals, and risk managers who work across sectors to protect the organization from legal exposure and regulatory penalties. It emphasizes analytical, investigative, and advisory functions within corporate and public-sector contexts.
[JF.09] Research and Development -- JF.09 Research and Development comprises roles that generate new knowledge, processes, products, or services and enhance existing offerings; workers in this family conduct scientific investigations, innovate, prototype, and test concepts to drive organizational growth, competitiveness, and technological advancement.
[JF.10] Engineering -- JF.10 Engineering groups occupations that design, develop, test, and maintain technical systems and infrastructure. It includes civil, mechanical, electrical, software and related engineering roles, covering planning, analysis, and implementation of solutions across industry and public sectors.
[JF.11] Customer Service and Support -- JF.11 encompasses positions that interact directly with customers to address inquiries, resolve problems, and provide product or service information, often through phone, email, chat or in-person channels. These roles focus on maintaining satisfaction, handling complaints, and supporting clients throughout the service lifecycle. The classification includes front-line representatives, help-desk agents, and support specialists across various industries.
[JF.12] Product Management -- JF.12 denotes the Product Management job family within the generic classification system. It covers roles responsible for overseeing product lifecycle, strategy, development, and market success, coordinating cross-functional teams to define requirements, prioritize features, and drive profitability.
[JF.13] Project/Program Management -- JF.13 Project/Program Management includes roles that plan, coordinate, and oversee projects or programs, managing resources, schedules, budgets, risks and stakeholder communication to ensure objectives are met on time and within scope.
[JF.14] Data and Analytics -- The Data and Analytics job family encompasses roles that gather, organize, and interpret data to support decision-making and drive organizational insight. It includes positions such as data engineers, business intelligence analysts, data scientists, and reporting specialists, all focused on turning raw information into actionable knowledge.
[JF.15] Quality Assurance -- JF.15 denotes the Quality Assurance job family within the generic classification system. Roles in this family focus on monitoring, testing, and improving products, services, and processes to ensure they meet established standards and regulations. Employees may develop inspection protocols, conduct audits, and recommend corrective actions.
[JF.16] Facilities and Administration -- The Facilities and Administration job family covers roles that manage the physical workplace and support services needed for organizational operations. Employees in this group are responsible for building maintenance, space planning, security, environmental health and safety, and the procurement and oversight of non-core services that keep the work environment functional and compliant.
[JF.17] Manufacturing/Production -- JF.17 refers to roles involved in the planning, execution, and oversight of manufacturing and production processes, including assembly line work, equipment operation, and quality control. It encompasses positions that transform raw materials into finished goods in various industries, ensuring efficiency, safety, and compliance with standards.
[JF.18] Health/Safety/Environment -- The JF.18 Health/Safety/Environment job family groups positions that develop, implement, and monitor policies and programs to protect employee health, ensure workplace safety, and manage environmental compliance, including risk assessments, regulatory reporting, and sustainability initiatives.
