[I2] ISO 20022 Business Areas -- I2 is the top-level grouping for ISO 20022 Business Areas, the broad functional domains that organize the standard's financial messaging taxonomy. It encompasses categories such as payments, securities, trade finance and cards, providing the umbrella under which more specific message categories are defined.
[I2.acmt] Account Management (acmt) -- I2.acmt groups ISO 20022 messages that support the creation, modification, status inquiry and closure of bank accounts. It enables institutions to exchange standardized requests and confirmations for retail or corporate account management, covering account opening, updating of details, and termination in a uniform electronic format.
[I2.admi] Administration (admi) -- The I2.admi category groups ISO 20022 messages that support administrative activities across the financial ecosystem, including account opening, closing, maintenance, authority delegation and control functions, and other routine operational tasks that enable institutions to manage relationships and resources efficiently.

[I2.caaa] Card Transaction (caaa) -- I2.caaa is the ISO 20022 message category for Card Transaction messages, identified by the "caaa" suffix. It covers a suite of messages used by issuers, acquirers and payment processors to handle card-based activities such as authorisation, clearing, settlement and related status updates across debit, credit and prepaid card schemes.
[I2.camt] Cash Management (camt) -- The I2.camt category groups ISO 20022 messages that support cash-management functions such as account reporting, balance inquiries and payment initiation. It includes the camt series of XML messages that enable banks and corporates to exchange cash-position, transaction and liquidity information in a standardized format.
[I2.colr] Collateral Management (colr) -- I2.colr designates the Collateral Management message category in the ISO 20022 Business Area taxonomy. It groups messages that support the creation, valuation, transfer, and monitoring of collateral used to secure financial transactions and exposures. This category enables standardized communication across institutions.
[I2.fxtr] Foreign Exchange Trade (fxtr) -- I2.fxtr is the ISO 20022 business-area code for the Foreign Exchange Trade message family. It groups messages that support the entire FX transaction lifecycle, including trade execution, confirmation, settlement and related reporting between participants in the foreign-exchange market.
[I2.pacs] Payments Clearing and Settlement (pacs) -- I2.pacs is the ISO 20022 Business Area category for payment clearing and settlement messages. It groups standards used to convey information for interbank transfers, direct debits, credit transfers and related reconciliation, enabling a common data model across the entire payment chain.
[I2.pain] Payments Initiation (pain) -- I2.pain is the ISO 20022 business-area code for payment-initiation messages, defining the standard formats used by banks and corporates to request, cancel or modify credit transfers and direct debits. These messages enable interoperable electronic initiation of payments across diverse payment systems.
[I2.reda] Reference Data (reda) -- I2.reda designates the Reference Data message category in ISO 20022, covering messages that distribute and maintain static data such as codes, identifiers, and master tables used across financial services. These messages support consistent data definitions for downstream processing and reporting.
[I2.remt] Payments Remittance Advice (remt) -- The I2.remt category groups ISO 20022 messages that convey remittance advice in payment transactions. It is used to provide detailed information to the creditor about the purpose, invoices or reference data associated with a payment, enabling reconciliation and record keeping.
[I2.secl] Securities Clearing (secl) -- I2.secl identifies the Securities Clearing business area in the ISO 20022 catalogue. It groups messages that support the clearing of securities transactions, including settlement instructions, confirmations and status notifications. These messages enable communication among clearing houses, custodians, and other post-trade participants.
[I2.semt] Securities Management (semt) -- I2.semt is the ISO 20022 message-category code for Securities Management, grouping messages that support the administration of financial instruments after issuance, including corporate-action processing, custody, settlement and position reporting. It enables standardized communication between issuers, custodians, agents and investors throughout the securities lifecycle.
[I2.sese] Securities Settlement (sese) -- The I2.sese category in ISO 20022 groups messages that support the processing and reconciliation of securities settlement activities, such as trade confirmation, settlement instruction and status reporting across markets. It covers the post-trade lifecycle for equities, bonds and other financial instruments, enabling standardized data exchange between participants.
[I2.setr] Securities Trade (setr) -- The I2.setr category groups ISO 20022 messages that support the full life-cycle of securities trades, including trade initiation, execution, confirmation, settlement and post-trade reporting. It provides a common syntax for banks, brokers and market infrastructures to exchange trade details and status updates across asset classes.
[I2.tsin] Trade Services Initiation (tsin) -- The I2.tsin entry groups ISO 20022 messages that initiate trade-related services such as letters of credit, guarantees, and documentary collections. These messages are used by corporates and banks to start the processing of trade finance transactions, conveying the necessary details to trigger subsequent steps in the workflow.
