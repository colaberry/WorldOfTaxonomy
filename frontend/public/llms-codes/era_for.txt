[EF] ERA FoR Divisions -- EF denotes the ERA Fields of Research (FoR) Divisions, the highest-level categories in Australia's research classification system. These divisions group detailed FoR codes into broad disciplines such as Natural Sciences, Engineering, Medical and Health Sciences, Agricultural Sciences, and the Social Sciences, providing a framework for national research assessment and funding allocation.

[EF.39] Education -- EF.39 Education covers research on learning, teaching and curriculum development across all educational levels and settings, including pedagogical theory, educational psychology, assessment, policy, administration and the use of technology to enhance instruction and outcomes.
