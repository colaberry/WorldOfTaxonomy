[exclusions] Exclusions (weapons, vehicles, ships, etc.) -- The exclusions entry lists categories of products that are not subject to the EU 2023/1230 machinery regulation, such as firearms, military weapons, motor vehicles, aircraft, and ships, together with related components and accessories. It defines essential health and safety objectives, prescribes risk-assessment procedures, and outlines conformity-assessment routes to ensure that machines placed on the market meet the prescribed protection standards.

It mandates documented procedures, traceability of components, regular audits and continuous improvement measures to ensure that all equipment meets the essential health and safety requirements set out in the regulation.
