[RL] RAL Color Ranges -- RL designates the RAL Color Ranges within the RAL color standard system, grouping individual RAL shades into broader hue families such as reds, blues, yellows, and greys. This classification helps users select related colors quickly for design, architecture, and industrial applications.
[RL.01] RAL 1xxx: Yellow Hues -- RL.01 designates the RAL 1xxx series, a range of standardized yellow hues used in paint, coatings and plastics. The series includes colors from bright lemon tones to deeper mustard shades, each identified by a four-digit code beginning with 1, facilitating consistent color specification across industries.
[RL.02] RAL 2xxx: Orange Hues -- RL.02 designates the RAL 2xxx series, which groups all official orange hues within the RAL color standard. The numbers run from 2000 to 2999 and cover shades from bright safety orange to deep terra cotta, commonly used in architecture, automotive finishes, and industrial safety markings.
[RL.03] RAL 3xxx: Red Hues -- RL.03 designates the RAL 3xxx series, covering all red hues in the RAL color system. These numbers, from 3000 to 3999, are standardized shades used for paints, coatings and design specifications across Europe and beyond.
[RL.04] RAL 4xxx: Violet Hues -- RL.04 identifies the RAL 4xxx series, a collection of violet hues ranging from deep purple to soft lilac. These colors are standardized for use in architecture, product design, and industrial applications, ensuring consistent matching across materials and manufacturers.
[RL.05] RAL 5xxx: Blue Hues -- RL.05 designates the RAL 5xxx series, a collection of standardized blue hues used across architecture, product design, and industrial applications. The category groups codes from 5000 to 5999, each with a specific name, visual reference, and technical data such as hex values and recommended uses.
[RL.06] RAL 6xxx: Green Hues -- RL.06 is the code for the RAL 6xxx series, which groups all green hues in the RAL color standard. The series assigns specific numbers to shades from deep forest green to bright lime, enabling precise color specification in design, architecture, and industry. It ensures consistent communication of green tones across manufacturers and applications
[RL.07] RAL 7xxx: Grey Hues -- RL.07 designates the RAL 7xxx series, a collection of standardized grey shades used in paints, coatings, and design specifications. The range includes tones from light silvers to deep charcoals, providing consistent colour references for industrial and creative applications.
[RL.08] RAL 8xxx: Brown Hues -- RL.08 designates the RAL 8xxx series, which groups brown hues within the RAL color system. This category includes a range of earthy tones such as RAL 8001 (Ochre brown) and RAL 8015 (Chestnut brown) used for paints, coatings, and design specifications. The series provides standardized references for consistent color matching across industries.
[RL.09] RAL 9xxx: White/Black Hues -- The RAL 9xxx series groups the outermost tones of the RAL palette, covering pure white, off-white, grey and black shades. These colors serve as neutral references for architectural finishes, industrial coatings and graphic design, offering a standardized set of light to dark neutrals.
[RL.10] RAL Design System Plus -- RL.10 refers to the RAL Design System Plus, an extension of the original RAL color palette that offers a comprehensive range of 1,625 solid colors defined by hue angle, chroma and lightness values. Developed for designers, architects and manufacturers, it provides a standardized reference for precise color communication across industries, ensuring consistency in product development, interior design and visual branding.
[RL.11] RAL Effect -- RL.11 identifies the RAL Effect group within the RAL colour system, covering special-effect finishes such as metallic, pearlescent and fluorescent shades. These colours are formulated to provide enhanced visual depth and sheen for decorative, automotive and industrial applications, extending the standard RAL Classic palette with premium surface effects.
[RL.12] RAL Plastics -- RL.12 designates the RAL Plastics category within the RAL color standard system. It groups standardized plastic materials and their associated hues, supplying manufacturers, designers, and specifiers with consistent color references for polymer products such as sheets, films, and molded components. This helps ensure uniform appearance across different production batches and suppliers.
