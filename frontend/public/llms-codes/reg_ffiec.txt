[is_risk] Risk Assessment -- The "is_risk" entry in the FFIEC IT Examination Handbook outlines how financial institutions must conduct a formal risk assessment of their information systems, identifying threats, vulnerabilities, and potential impacts on confidentiality, integrity, and availability.
