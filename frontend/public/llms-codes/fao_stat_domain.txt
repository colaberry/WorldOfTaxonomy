[FD] FAOSTAT Domains -- FD denotes the FAOSTAT Domains category, grouping the thematic areas and datasets within the FAO's statistical database, such as agriculture, fisheries, forestry and related socioeconomic indicators.
[FD.01] Production: Crops and Livestock Products -- FD.01 in the FAO Statistical Database Domain Categories refers to the collection of statistical data on the production of both crops and livestock products, encompassing quantities harvested, yields, and output values for commodities such as grains, vegetables, meat, milk, and eggs, used for monitoring agricultural performance and informing policy.
[FD.02] Production: Value of Production -- FD.02 represents the monetary value of agricultural production as recorded in the FAO statistical database. It quantifies the total gross value of crops, livestock, fisheries and related outputs in current market prices, enabling economic monitoring and comparison.

[FD.04] Trade: Detailed Trade Matrix -- FD.04 is the FAO Detailed Trade Matrix, a dataset that records bilateral import and export quantities and values for agricultural commodities by country, year and product classification. It enables analysts to examine trade patterns, assess market access and monitor changes in food supply chains.
[FD.05] Food Balances: Food Supply -- FAO code FD.05 represents the Food Balances data set that details the national supply of food commodities measured in terms of availability for consumption. It aggregates production, imports, exports, stock changes and other adjustments to estimate per-capita food supply and its composition across different food groups.
[FD.06] Food Balances: Food Security -- FAO's FD.06 category aggregates national food balance sheet data to assess the overall availability of food commodities and the degree of food security in a country. It tracks quantities of food produced, imported, exported, processed and consumed, providing per-capita indicators that help monitor nutrition adequacy and vulnerability to shortages.
[FD.07] Prices: Producer Prices -- FD.07 in the FAO Statistical Database refers to data on producer prices for agricultural commodities, covering the prices received by producers for outputs at the farm gate. The series track changes over time and are used for market analysis, policy assessment and price monitoring.
[FD.08] Prices: Consumer Price Indices -- FD.08 covers data on consumer price indices, which track changes over time in the prices of a representative basket of goods and services purchased by households. These statistics are used to monitor inflation, compare cost-of-living trends across regions, and inform economic policy and market analysis.
[FD.09] Land Use -- FD.09 Land Use records how land is allocated among agricultural and non-agricultural purposes, detailing areas under crops, pastures, forests, and other uses to support monitoring of food security, resource management and environmental reporting.
[FD.10] Emissions: Agriculture -- FD.10 covers greenhouse-gas emissions that stem from agricultural activities, including methane from livestock and rice paddies, nitrous oxide from fertilizer use and soil management, and carbon dioxide released by land-use change and biomass burning. The data help assess agriculture's contribution to climate change and monitor mitigation efforts.
[FD.11] Emissions: Land Use -- FD.11 records greenhouse-gas emissions that arise from land-use activities, including deforestation, afforestation, forest degradation and agricultural practices, expressed in CO₂-equivalent units. It provides data for monitoring how changes in land use contribute to overall emissions in the FAO statistical framework.
[FD.12] Fertilizers -- FD.12 is the code for the Fertilizers category in the FAO Statistical Database Domain. It aggregates data on the production, consumption, trade and stocks of mineral and organic fertilizers used in agriculture, facilitating monitoring of nutrient inputs worldwide.
[FD.13] Pesticides -- FD.13 in the FAO Statistical Database covers data on pesticides, encompassing the production, import, export, domestic supply and consumption of insecticides, herbicides, fungicides and other crop-protective chemicals, as well as information on application rates, usage trends and losses in agricultural and related sectors.
[FD.14] Investment: Government Expenditure -- FD.14 refers to data on public investment, specifically the amount of money spent by governments on capital formation and infrastructure projects, as recorded in the FAO statistical database. It includes expenditures on agricultural facilities, research, and rural development, measured in monetary units.
[FD.15] Investment: Credit to Agriculture -- FD.15 denotes the category of financial flows that represent credit extended to the agricultural sector, including loans, guarantees and other financing instruments. It is used in the FAO Statistical Database to track investment in agriculture and assess funding sources for farm productivity and development.
[FD.16] Population: Annual Population -- FD.16 refers to the FAO statistical category for annual population figures, covering total counts of people in a country or region for each calendar year. The data are used to contextualize agricultural and food-security analyses by linking demographic trends with production, consumption and resource needs.
