[GC] GCC Tariff Sections -- GC denotes the GCC Tariff Sections within the GCC Common Customs Tariff, grouping customs duties and regulatory provisions by broad product categories for the Gulf Cooperation Council member states. It serves as a structural framework for applying uniform rates and rules across the region's trade.
[GC.01] Live Animals -- The GCC Common Customs Tariff code GC.01 applies to all live animals imported, exported or temporarily admitted, covering mammals, birds, reptiles, amphibians and fish kept alive for breeding, consumption, exhibition or research. It includes livestock, pets, zoo and wildlife specimens, and any related accessories required to keep the animals alive during transport.
[GC.02] Vegetable Products -- GC.02 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff applies to vegetable products imported into Gulf Cooperation Council markets, encompassing fresh, chilled, frozen, dried and processed vegetables, as well as vegetable seeds, pulp, extracts and related by-products, classified according to their nature and level of processing.
[GC.03] Fats and Oils -- This entry includes animal and vegetable fats and oils, covering crude, refined and hydrogenated forms, their fractions and specialty products such as essential fatty acids, while excluding items classified elsewhere for food, pharmaceutical or cosmetic use.
[GC.04] Prepared Foodstuffs -- GC.04 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff classifies prepared foodstuffs, covering cooked, processed or ready-to-eat items such as canned goods, frozen meals and other foods that have been altered from their natural state for consumption.
[GC.05] Mineral Products -- GC.05 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff groups all mineral products, including raw ores, industrial minerals, cement, glass, ceramics and related manufactured items derived from natural stone or mineral substances, and sets the duty rates and regulatory requirements for their import and export across GCC member states.
[GC.06] Chemical Products -- GC.06 under the GCC Common Customs Tariff groups all chemical products, ranging from basic inorganic compounds to complex organic formulations used in industry, agriculture and consumer goods. The entry applies a standard GCC duty rate unless a specific subheading provides a different rate or exemption, and it requires compliance with the Gulf Cooperation Council's safety, labeling and import documentation regulations.
[GC.07] Plastics -- GC.07 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff groups all polymeric materials and articles made of plastics, including raw polymers, molded or shaped plastics, and components such as sheets, rods, plates and finished goods, irrespective of their specific chemical composition, for the purpose of uniform customs duties across Gulf Cooperation Council member states.
[GC.08] Hides and Leather -- GC.08 covers raw and prepared hides, skins and their derivatives, including untanned animal pelts, split or whole, as well as tanned or dressed leather used for clothing, upholstery, footwear and other manufactured goods, except those specifically classified under other chapters for specialized leather products.
[GC.09] Wood -- GC.09 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff covers wood in its various forms, such as logs, sawn timber, veneers and other processed wood products used for construction, furniture and manufacturing. The entry groups unseasoned and seasoned wood, whether rough-sawn or planed, and applies the standard GCC duty rates and any applicable trade measures. Sub-headings further differentiate by dimensions, treatment and end-use.
[GC.10] Paper -- GC.10 covers paper and paperboard, whether uncoated, coated, or impregnated, in rolls, sheets, or other forms, intended for printing, writing, packaging or other uses, but excludes goods classified elsewhere such as newsprint, cardboard or specialized paper products. This code applies to the basic commodity before further processing or conversion.
[GC.11] Textiles -- GC.11 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff classifies all textile articles and raw materials, including yarns, fabrics, knitted and woven goods, and finished textile products such as garments and home textiles.
[GC.12] Footwear -- GC.12 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff applies to all footwear imported into GCC member states, encompassing shoes, boots, sandals and sports shoes and is further subdivided by material composition and intended use. The code is used for customs duty assessment and trade statistics across the Gulf Cooperation Council.
[GC.13] Ceramics and Glass -- GC.13 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff covers all ceramic and glass products, including porcelain, earthenware, terracotta, household glassware, laboratory glass, and decorative items, subject to the regional import duties and regulations. It groups these materials for uniform tariff treatment across GCC member states.
[GC.14] Base Metals -- GC.14 covers primary base metals and their simple alloys, including unrefined copper, aluminum, lead, zinc, nickel, tin and iron, as well as ingots and billets produced for further processing. The entry applies to goods in their raw or semi-finished form before fabrication into finished products, and is used to assess common customs duties across GCC member states.
[GC.15] Machinery -- GC.15 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff covers a broad range of machinery, including industrial, agricultural, and construction equipment, as well as parts and accessories essential for their operation, but excludes electrical appliances and consumer electronics, which are classified elsewhere.
[GC.16] Vehicles -- GC.16 in the GCC Common Customs Tariff groups all motor vehicles, including passenger cars, commercial trucks, buses and related chassis, along with their major components and parts such as engines, gearboxes and axles, that are imported or exported within the Gulf Cooperation Council member states.
